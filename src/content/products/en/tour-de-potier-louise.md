**Louise** is a powerful, quiet, configurable, disassemblable and repairable pottery wheel, entirely designed and manufactured in France.

### Features

- **Geared motor** 550W at 1450 rpm (IMO Jeambrun) with variable speed from 0 to 290 rpm
- **Wheel head** Ø295 mm aluminium, cast by [Fonderie de Roanne](https://roanne-fonderie.fr/), scored at centimetre intervals, Shimpo compatible
- **Spindle** stainless steel 25 mm, machined in-house, removable
- **Reversible rotation** with direction switch
- **Controls**: wired foot pedal + on/off buttons and speed control + LED indicator
- **Splash pan** and wheel head removable (8 mm bolts, 13 mm wrench included)
- **Adjustable height** via four feet
- **Bearing** bronze bushing, press-fitted

### Manufacturing

Each wheel is made to order in our workshop, with a lead time of approximately one month.
