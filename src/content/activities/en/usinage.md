## Our machining expertise

At COMUA, machining relies on proven conventional machines — no CNC. Precision to one hundredth of a millimetre on 2 or 3 axes.

### Our equipment

- **Ernault-Somua HN350 lathe** — a robust parallel lathe fitted with a digital readout (DRO) for real-time dimensional control
- **Somua Z1C universal-head milling machine** — a versatile mill enabling 2- or 3-axis work across a wide variety of angles and operations

These benchmark French machines, combined with our experience, allow us to achieve tolerances of one hundredth of a millimetre without CNC.

### Our approach

Every part is studied to optimise the manufacturing process while guaranteeing the required dimensional quality and surface finish. We work closely with our clients to deliver tailored technical solutions.

### Applications

- Replacement mechanical parts
- Prototyping
- Tooling and machining fixtures
- Special-purpose machine components
- Parts for the food-processing and chemical industries
