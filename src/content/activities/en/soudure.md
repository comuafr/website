## Our welding expertise

COMUA offers a full welding and sheet-metal service, suited to anything from small repairs to complex structural assemblies.

Our command of multiple welding processes (TIG, MIG/MAG, arc) allows us to work on steel and stainless steel across a wide range of thicknesses.

### Our sheet-metal equipment

- **Bombled roller** — 1000 mm width capacity, up to 3 mm thickness, for rolling sheet metal and producing shells and tubes

### Our approach

The quality of our welds rests on thorough part preparation and systematic visual inspection of every bead. We prioritise both the strength and the appearance of every assembly.

### Applications

- Frames and metal structures
- Railings, gates and architectural metalwork
- Machine and equipment repair
- Custom-made parts
- Pipework and fittings
